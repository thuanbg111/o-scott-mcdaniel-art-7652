Governance & Controls

O Scott McDaniel Art maintains bylaws, conflict-of-interest policies, and financial oversight to comply with U.S. nonprofit requirements. Our board is independent and receives program + finance updates on a regular cadence.

Board members review budgets, fundraising activity, and program performance to ensure charitable resources are used correctly.

Financial Practices
